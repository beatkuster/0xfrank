# 0xFrank

0xFranken is a blockchain-based payment solution designed for small retail stores that want to accept digital payments without intermediaries or transaction fees. Merchants simply connect their wallet, enter the amount, and show a QR code — the customer scans it and confirms the payment in seconds. Both merchants and customers hold Frankencoin (ZCHF), a Swiss franc-backed stablecoin, so there is no currency risk or volatility to worry about. Beyond payments, both parties can earn yield on their ZCHF balance through a built-in savings account, turning idle funds into a passive income stream. 0xFranken makes crypto payments as simple as a card terminal, but open, permissionless, and free.
